About:

Hello, my name is Solange, I'm an artist, a wife, and a mother of two. Being born in a Brazilian countryside city, I always admired the beauty of nature. In my childhood, I always liked to climb a tree to eat fresh fruit and see the beautiful landscapes, and a small river flowing near my house.

Since there, even though my family moved to a big city, I always carry the vivid memories of the smell of the cotton fields, the sound of birds chirping, and the colors in the sky on the sunsets.

I moved to Chicago with my husband to work in the year 1998, and the city enchanted me with its beautiful and cosmopolitan atmosphere, but I always wanted to show the beauty through my eyes, so Solange Art Studios was born.
